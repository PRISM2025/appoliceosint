üí° Tips for Effective OSINT Learning

  • Start with Basics: Begin with the beginner courses before moving to advanced topics.
  • Practice Regularly: Apply what you learn through practical exercises and real-world scenarios.
  • Take Notes: Document tools, techniques, and important concepts as you learn.
  • Join Communities: Engage with OSINT communities on Twitter, Reddit, and Discord.
  • Stay Updated: OSINT tools and techniques evolve rapidly - keep learning continuously.
  • Ethics First: Always conduct OSINT activities ethically and within legal boundaries.
  • Build a Toolkit: Create your own collection of favorite OSINT tools and resources.
  • Document Your Process: Practice documenting your investigation process for reporting.
