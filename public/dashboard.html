Welcome to the OSINT Tools Platform of the Andhra Pradesh Police Department. This platform provides a suite of open-source intelligence tools designed to assist law enforcement and investigative agencies in gathering, analyzing, and utilizing publicly available information for various purposes.

1

Comprehensive OSINT Platform Features:

  • Comprehensive Toolset: Access a wide range of OSINT tools covering social media analysis, domain intelligence, breach data investigation, and more.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Navigate through an intuitive interface that simplifies the process of using complex OSINT tools.
  • Secure Access: Ensure that only authorized personnel can access sensitive tools and data through robust authentication mechanisms.
  • Regular Updates: Stay informed with the latest updates and enhancements to the tools, ensuring you have access to cutting-edge capabilities.

Whether you're conducting investigations, performing background checks, or gathering intelligence, our OSINT Tools Platform is here to support your efforts in maintaining public safety and security.
